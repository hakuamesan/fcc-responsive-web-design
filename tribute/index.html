Defining Yoga

Yoga, as described in the classical text 'Yoga Sutra' by Patanjali, is a way of calming the mental disturbances so that you can let your true self emerge.

This can be achieved in 8 easy steps, namely:

Step 1: Yam

Social restrains

Step 2: Niyam

Step 3: Asana

Step 4: Pranayama

Step 5: Pratyahara

Step 6: Dharana

Step 7: Dhyana

Step 8: Samadhi

  • Yoga is:
  • A method for everyone irrespective of age, gender, class, money, status...
  • A means to improve yourself by gaining control of yourself
  • A way to acquire good health and inner peace
  • Easy!
  • Yoga is NOT:
  • A form of exercise only
  • A religious or cult organization
  • required to buy expensive equipments, clothing or classes
  • Same as going to gym

Keep calm and do Yoga!
